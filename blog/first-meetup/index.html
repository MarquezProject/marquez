Join Us in October for Our First Meetup

August 14, 2023 · 2 min read
Michael Robinson
Marquez Community Manager

Join us on Thursday, October 5th, from 5:30-8:30 pm to learn about the present and future of the Marquez project.

You will:

  • meet other members of the community,
  • learn about the project’s goals and fundamental design,
  • hear about the project's journey from WeWork to OpenLineage adoption to Astronomer,
  • brainstorm about data lineage,
  • participate in a discussion about the future of the project, &
  • submit your first PR to the project. If that's not enough, there will also be stickers! Bring your appetite and your ideas and vision for Marquez!

Food will be provided, and the meetup is open to all. Don't miss this opportunity to influence the direction of the project! We hope to see you there. Please sign up to let us know you're coming.

Time, Place & Format​

Date: October 5, 2023
Format: In-person
Time: 5:30-8:30 pm ET
Address: Astronomer, 8 California Street, 7th Floor, San Francisco, CA 94111

Getting There​

The Astronomer SF office is in the Financial District at the corner of California and Drumm Streets, catty-cornered from the Market/Drumm Street cable car stop.

Getting In​

An Astronomer team member in the lobby will direct you to the Astronomer offices on the seventh floor.

Hope to see you there!​
